Mango

You seriously cannot go wrong with a good mango. Only thing that makes mangoes better is when they are frozen. The perfect treat anytime of the year, I could and I do, eat frozen mangoes by the bag full. I don't know what I would do without this sweet treat in my life. Without a doubt, frozen mangoes are a 10/10

Strawberry

Strawberries are always a classic; however, they are kind of subpar in frozen form. The berries are too big, too hard to munch on, they always get stuck up in the blender, and often give me a brain freeze. I would say that although I like strawberries, the frozen form doesn't deserve much better then a 4/10

Banana

Ok, bananas can be controversial, you either love them or you hate them. for me, I love them and freezing them only enhances thier best qualities. Frozen bananas are perfect in smoothies or just on a hot summer day. cover them in chocolate, and you've got banana perfection! I will say, they can be too hard to consume if cut into bigger chunks, but a few more cuts make them the perfect frozen bite. a solid 8/10

blueberry

In my personal opinion, bluberries can be a hit or a miss. It can be hard to get a container of blueberries with every bite being sweet, berry goodness. Fortunatly, frozen blueberries eliminate this problem and help make it enjoyable to eat blueberries by the spoonful. Frozen blueberries can enhance cereal, yogurt, and much more, making them the perfect addition for your freezer! 9/10

Peach

There's nothing better then a fresh juicy peach, you really can't go wrong; however, peaches are one of those fruits that I personally feel taste better fresh. Don't get me wrong, they are good as a frozen fruit, and make great additions to smoothies, but I much prefer to consume peaches in a ripe, juicy form, skin and all. I'd given frozen peaches a 6/10

Raspberry

Raspberries to me, are a fruit delicacy. They are such a good fruit, but why oh why do they have to be so expensive?! Frozen raspberries are no different, they are a bank breaker, however sometimes you just need to splurge because they are so dang good. Frozen rasperries might be at the top of my list if it weren't for the price, so I'm going to have to give them an 8/10
